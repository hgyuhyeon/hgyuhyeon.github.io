Overview
Solar power prediction is difficult to predict power because of the locations of the sun. This even doesn't have a constant time of the day depending on the season. To apply time series models for the prediction, we used 3 models and compared each of its result.
Tech stack
Python, Pandas
  • Used Pandas for efficient data preprocessing and analysis.
  • 효과적인 데이터 전처리 및 분석을 위해 Pandas 사용.
Contributions
  • Pre-processed large weather and solar power datasets using Pandas, ensuring accuracy and eliminating noise.
  • Applied the ARIMA model to predict solar power generation, achieving a 5% improvement in forecast accuracy using differencing.
    • ARIMA doesn't have Seasonality while other models(SARIMA, LSTM) have, it had biggest RMSE error value among them.
    • Applied differencing 2 times to minimize RMSE error value for the data has Stationarity and Seasonality.
  • Pandas를 사용하여 대규모 날씨 및 태양광 데이터 전처리, 데이터 정확성 보장 및 잡음 제거.
  • ARIMA 모델을 사용하여 태양광 발전량을 예측, 차분을 통해 5% 예측 정확도 향상.
    • ARIMA모델은 비교군인 SARIMA, LSTM과 달리 계절성이 없어 오차가 컸음.
    • 차분을 2회 적용, ARIMA 모델로도 계절성을 최대한 살릴 수 있도록 함.
